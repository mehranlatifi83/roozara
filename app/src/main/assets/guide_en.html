User Guide

Everything you need to know about Roozara

Sleep Schedule

Tap the schedule card on the main screen to set your sleep time and wake time. Then flip the switch to activate.

  • Switch ON: At your sleep time every night, the phone silences, internet is blocked, and the lock screen activates automatically. A reminder is sent 15 minutes before.
  • Switch OFF: Nothing happens automatically. Your saved times are kept for when you re-enable it.
  • Alarms survive phone restarts — they are restored automatically after reboot.
You must set both a sleep time and a wake time before enabling the schedule.

Challenge Mode

Controls how hard it is to dismiss the wake alarm — and the early-exit button. Set it from the main screen.

  • Simple: Tap a button to confirm you're awake. Best if you just want schedule enforcement without extra friction.
  • Math: Solve a math problem. Difficulty increases with each wrong answer:
    • Easy: single multiplication (e.g. 6 × 7)
    • Medium: multiply then add/subtract (e.g. (4 × 8) + 19)
    • Hard: product of two multiplications (e.g. (3 × 6) × (2 × 9))
  • Memory: A random 5-digit sequence is displayed for 4 seconds, then hidden. Type it from memory. Tap Retry to get a new sequence.

Early Exit

After half the sleep window has passed, an Early Exit button appears on the lock screen. To use it, you must pass the same challenge as the wake alarm. It is not a free bypass.

Wake Alarm

At your wake time, an alarm rings and the challenge screen appears. The alarm will not stop until the challenge is completed — there is no snooze or dismiss button.

  • If you navigate away, the challenge screen remains reachable from the notification shade and the Recents switcher.
  • If you are already on the lock screen when wake time arrives, no duplicate notification is sent.

Alarm Sound

Tap Alarm Sound on the main screen to choose your wake alarm audio:

  • System ringtone: Browse your phone's built-in alarm sounds.
  • Audio file: Pick any audio file from your storage.

If nothing is selected, the system default alarm is used.

Draw Over Other Apps

An optional permission that changes how the lock screen appears:

  • Granted: The lock screen appears instantly on top of any app — including the home screen — the moment your sleep time arrives.
  • Not granted: A high-priority full-screen notification is used instead. This may not cover the foreground app on all devices.
Granting this permission is strongly recommended for reliable sleep locking.

Water Reminders

Open the Water tab at the bottom of the screen to set up hydration reminders.

  • Set your meal times (breakfast, lunch, dinner) — reminders are automatically spaced around them for optimal hydration windows.
  • 8 reminders are sent throughout the day.
  • Switch ON: Reminders are active and rescheduled automatically every day.
  • Switch OFF: All water reminders are cancelled. Your settings are saved.
  • Delivered as an overlay popup (if permission granted) or a notification.

Permissions Explained

  • Post Notifications: Required for sleep, wake, and water alerts.
  • Schedule Exact Alarms: Required for the sleep timer and water reminders to fire at the exact right time.
  • Do Not Disturb access: Required to silence your ringer at bedtime and restore it at wake time.
  • Draw Over Other Apps: Optional — for a true full-screen lock overlay.
  • Full-Screen Intents (Android 14+): Required for the wake alarm to appear over the lock screen.
