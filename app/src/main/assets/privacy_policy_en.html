Privacy Policy

App: Salemzi  |  Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Salemzi is a sleep and wellness app. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Salemzi does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter (sleep schedule, meal times, preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never shared with third parties.

Permissions Explained

  • Do Not Disturb access — used to silence your phone during sleep mode. We do not read your notification content.
  • VPN (network blocking) — used to block internet access during sleep mode. No traffic is logged, inspected, or transmitted anywhere.
  • Display over other apps — used to show the sleep lock screen over any foreground app when bedtime arrives.
  • Schedule exact alarms — used to fire your sleep and wake-up schedule at the correct time.
  • Full-screen intent — used to show the wake-up screen when your alarm fires.
  • Receive Boot Completed — used to restore your schedule after the device restarts.
  • Post notifications — used to show sleep reminders and wake-up notifications.

Third-Party Services

Salemzi does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Salemzi is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated date at the top of this document will reflect the change.
